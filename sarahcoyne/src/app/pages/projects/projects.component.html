My Projects

Some current and past projects that I have worked on in the Sensory Lab, more details can be found on my resume and CV!

Volitlile Characterization with GCMS and SPME

In 2024, I presented on GM-MS and SMPE characterization of 18 volatile compounds of treated and untreated pulse flours of white kidney, cranberry, and chickpea cultivars. I continued to be a part of this project through 2025. This project allowed me to get comfortable using anylitical equimpent.

Consumer and Non-Comsumer Pulse Products Focus Group

In 2025, I presented data collected from a focus group I assisted with on consumer preferences and perceptions of pulse based products on the market to better understand the hurdles that product developers face today. I was able to better understand different methods of consumer data collection.

Industry and Departemetal Collaborations

I have also helped on a handful of industry projects and department collaborations including a consumer study on fermented corn pancakes done in partnership with the horticulture department!
